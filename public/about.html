About

This site was made for students, parents, teachers and all other community members to have clean and consistent access to grades at the Illinois Mathematics and Sciecne Academy. Data was aquired through a 2017 FOIA Request, by a '18 student. Data from 2018-2020 was gathered separately in collaboration with the Office of Institutional Research.

Note this is not an official site. Data was attained in collaboration with the Office of Institutional Research. If you find an error, please report it.
